Can I actually prove what I learned to an employer?
Yes. Every project you ship gets a verified "proof of skill" credential with a shareable link — reviewers can see the working project, not just a certificate claiming you finished a video.
What happens to my streak and history if I downgrade to Free?
Your streak, completed courses, and proof-of-skill history are yours permanently, on any plan. Downgrading only limits daily AI tutor sessions and new-course access going forward.
Do live study rooms cost extra?
No — community study rooms are included on every plan, including Free. Pro and above unlock creating your own private rooms for a study group.
Is there a discount for students or teams?
Verified students get 50% off Pro. Teams of 5 or more should use the Team plan, which includes shared progress reporting and volume pricing at higher seat counts.
